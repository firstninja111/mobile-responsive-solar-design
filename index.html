Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my solar roof system last?

You should expect a 25-year plus life on your solar roof system.

How does a solar installation affect my home insurance or mortgage?

Mortgage providers and insurance companies rarely penalise you for having solar on your home. They will probably ask to see copies of the completion certificates for the installation.

What impact will solar PV have on the future value of my home?

Multiple surveys carried out over the past five years have demonstrated that installing solar on the roof of your home is likely to increase the value of your home.

How confident is the solar savings report generated from PVROOF?

Data and information is derived from public sources. PVRoof, in providing the data and information, believe that the information it uses comes from reliables sources, but do not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of this information, which is subject to change without notice, and nothing in our report should be construed assuch a guarantee.

What if I cannot afford to buy solar?

From our report, we recommend other finance option such as loans or lease options. Speak with our accredited solar representative about your finances, and he/she should be able to recommend the right option for you.

How big should be roof be for solar?

A standard 4kW to 5kW solar system would require approximately 28 to 35 square meters of clean roof space. Based on the recommended solar size we suggest for you, our accredited solar representative will conduct a roof survey to ensure your roof is safe, and has the required area for installation.
