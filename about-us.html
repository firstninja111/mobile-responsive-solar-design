About out solar estimator

Our Solar estimator is a global tool that plays a vital role in helping homeowners decarbonise and switch to affordable renewable solar power on their roofs.

Built in over 5yrs+, this tool has been supported by academia (Imperial College London), UK Government (ESRC IAA Grant), and the private sector.

Our solar estimate is a global tool that holds over 1million unique dataset.

Why you should get solar on your roof

The price of electricity has now increased by two-three folds as a result of market forces, and the conflict in Eastern Europe.

As a result, there has been a massive demand for solar amongst homeowners worldwide to capitalise on the now low cost of solar equipment, most most importantly to earn needed savings.
